About Me

Hello, my name is Della, and I am an undergraduate student majoring in Information Technology with a strong passion for fullstack development. I believe that technology is not just about writing code, it’s about creating meaningful solutions that can improve lives, solve real problems, and inspire progress.

My journey into the world of web development began with a fascination for how things work on the internet. Starting with basic HTML and CSS, I quickly moved on to JavaScript, then explored backend frameworks like PHP, and database management with MySQL. I find great satisfaction in bridging frontend aesthetics with robust backend logic, turning ideas into complete, functional, and user-friendly applications.

For me, being a developer means more than technical ability. It means thinking critically, designing with empathy, and delivering value through every project. I always strive to combine logic, creativity, and human-centered thinking in the solutions I build.

Beyond coding, I’m a strong believer in continuous learning and adaptability. I enjoy collaborating with others, exchanging ideas, and learning from different perspectives. I thrive in environments where teamwork, innovation, and curiosity are encouraged.

My goal is to grow as a developer who not only builds software, but also builds impact — through clean code, purposeful design, and meaningful contributions to the people and communities I work with.
